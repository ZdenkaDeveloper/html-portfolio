Donkey

Summary

Donkey is a fictional character created by William Steig and adapted by DreamWorks Animation for the Shrek franchise.

Biography

  1. Shrek

    2001

    • Made his debut at a sale of mythical characters from old fairy tales, being sold to evil Lord Farquaad's knights.
    • Donkey's special power was his ability to talk.
    • Following a lucky escape, he befriends Shrek.

  2. Shrek 2

    2004

    • Donkey first appears in the film, having been remaining at Shrek's house while Shrek and Fiona were away on thei honeymoon.
    • He accompanied Shrek and Fiona to the Far Far Away kingdom and repeatedly asks, "Are we there yet?" Shrek and Fiona keep answering no.
